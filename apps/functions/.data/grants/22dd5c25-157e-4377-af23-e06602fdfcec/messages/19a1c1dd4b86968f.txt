Feed the Raptor, Not the Pigeon – How Hierarchical Chunking + Contextual Retrieval Turns a Plain-Old RAG Into a Carnivore Ercin Dedeoğlu explores how a system named Feed Raptor Pigeon applies hierarchical chunking to transform flat document segments into layered, tree-organised elements – enhancing the precision and context-awareness of RAG pipelines. The article highlights how chunking strategies can significantly improve LLM performance on complex knowledge tasks by structuring retrieval in multiple abstraction levels..
